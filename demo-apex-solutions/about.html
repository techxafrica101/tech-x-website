Who We Are

Apex Solutions Ltd is a leading professional services company based in Zambia, dedicated to helping businesses achieve sustainable growth and operational excellence. Since our establishment in 2016, we have been at the forefront of business transformation, combining deep local market knowledge with international best practices.

Our team of experienced consultants, project managers, and digital specialists work closely with clients across various industries to deliver tailored solutions that drive measurable results. We understand the unique challenges facing businesses in the Zambian market and leverage this insight to create strategies that work.

From small and medium enterprises to large corporations, we have successfully partnered with organizations to streamline operations, implement digital solutions, and achieve their strategic objectives.
