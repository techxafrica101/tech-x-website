Who We Are

Tech-X is a Zambian-based innovation company dedicated to solving real African problems using cutting-edge technology. We focus on creating data-driven solutions that enhance efficiency and deliver measurable impact.

Our vision-driven and youth-powered team combines deep understanding of African markets with global technology standards. We specialize in building data platforms, web solutions, and digital tools that work within existing workflows rather than requiring complete organizational restructuring.

From small and medium enterprises to large institutions, we have successfully partnered with organizations to streamline operations, implement digital solutions, and achieve their strategic objectives.
