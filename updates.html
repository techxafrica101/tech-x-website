StatFlow Platform Architecture Complete

We've finalized the core architecture for StatFlow, our real-time data analytics platform. November 28, 2025 Building

Agri-Tech Platform Development Begins

We've started development on our comprehensive agri-tech platform, designed to connect smallholder farmers with markets, weather data, and financial services. The platform addresses three critical gaps we've identified in the agricultural value chain.

First, information asymmetry—farmers often lack access to real-time market prices and weather forecasts. Second, market access—connecting producers directly with buyers reduces intermediary costs. Third, financial inclusion—integrating mobile money and micro-lending services.

The technical architecture leverages SMS, USSD, and mobile app interfaces to ensure accessibility across different technology adoption levels in rural communities.
