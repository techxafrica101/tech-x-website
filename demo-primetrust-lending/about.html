Who We Are

PrimeTrust Lending Services is a registered micro-lending institution based in Zambia, dedicated to providing accessible financial solutions to individuals and small businesses across the country.

Since our establishment, we have been committed to bridging the gap between traditional banking and the financial needs of everyday Zambians. Our focus on transparency, speed, and customer service has made us a trusted name in the lending industry.
