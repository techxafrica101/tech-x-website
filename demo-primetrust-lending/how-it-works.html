How It Works

Our simple 4-step process gets you the funds you need quickly and transparently.

1

Apply Online

Fill out our simple online application form with your personal and financial information. The process takes just 5-10 minutes.

Required Information:

  • • Personal details
  • • Loan amount needed
  • • Purpose of loan
  • • Repayment period
2

Review & Approval

Our team reviews your application and verifies your information. We'll contact you within 24 hours with our decision.

We Check:

  • • Identity verification
  • • Income assessment
  • • Collateral evaluation
  • • Credit worthiness
3

Disbursement

Once approved, we'll disburse the funds directly to your bank account or mobile money within 24-48 hours.

Disbursement Options:

  • • Bank transfer
  • • Mobile money
  • • Cash pickup
  • • Direct deposit
4

Monthly Repayment

Make your monthly payments on the 1st of every month. We offer multiple convenient payment methods for your ease.

Payment Methods:

  • • Bank transfer
  • • Mobile money
  • • Office payment
  • • Auto-debit

What You Need to Apply

Make sure you have these documents ready before starting your application.

Personal Documents

  • Valid National Registration Card (NRC)
  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Proof of residence (utility bill)

Financial Documents

  • Latest payslip or income proof
  • Bank statements (3 months)
  • Employment letter (if employed)

Collateral/Surety

  • Guarantor with valid NRC
  • Collateral documentation
  • Guarantor's income proof
